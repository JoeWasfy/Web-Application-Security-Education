Here's how to get the most out of the Web Security Educational Challenges

Welcome to our Security Learning Tool! Starting with web security might seem hard, but don't worry. We've made a step-by-step guide to help you. Go through the tasks as you like. If something's tough, you can skip it and come back later. As you learn more, you'll feel better about it.

We have challengess where you can try what you've learned. Try to finish all the tasks in the Web application security education. But take your time and understand everything without just looking for answers.
